Swim your heart out

Swimming is one of the most strenous activities as it
involves multiple compound muscles to work efficiently
in water. Whether it is for fitness or leisure we ensure
that our pools have crystal clear water. Our changing
rooms are spotless and clean. Our friendly staff is
available to welcome you and make the whole
experience exceptional.

  • Our pools vary from; 25m to 30m in length.

  • Maximum depth of 7.5ft as safety is our number one priority.

  • Efficient water treatment system – suitable chlorine level as there is no compromise in the standard of hygiene

  • Seperate pool for kids.(4-14 years old)

  • Our pool is temperature controlled in all seasons as per international standards.

  • Swimming lessons for beginners by professional coaches
  • Indoor and fully covered pools to respect the privacy of our members.

  • Separate lockers, changing room shower rooms & towels available for each pool.

  • Seperate timing for ladies and gents to respect the privacy of male and female alike.

  • Steam, Sauna & Jaccuzzi baths added facilities with Swimming Pool

    Sauna & Steam

    Sauna and Steam are both ancient practices for rejuvenating the body and mind. Relax, unwind and take your time to rest your body. Rid away of aching muscles and come out as a fully refreshed person.
    Now and then, sprinkle a ladle of water over the hot sauna stones and feel the unique, tangling sensation as wave after wave of heat penetrates deep into your skin. The Sauna helps you open up your pores, enabling perspiration that provides the necessary release of salts and other bodily enzymes for a more relaxed you.

    What we offer at our Sauna is:

  • Temperature: 82 - 93°C
  • Humidity: 10-15% (dry heat)
  • Maximum use per sitting: 15 minutes
  • Maximum use per day: 30 minutes
